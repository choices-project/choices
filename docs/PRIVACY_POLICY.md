- **Control:** `trackFeedActivity` setting
- **Default:** OFF (you must enable it)

- **Why:** To improve the platform and fix issues
- **Control:** `collectAnalytics` setting

## Privacy Controls

You have **16 granular privacy controls** to manage your data. All default to OFF (maximum privacy).

### Data Collection Controls

#### 1. Location Data (`collectLocationData`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We collect and store your city, state, and ZIP code
- **When OFF:** Location lookups are temporary; we don't save your location
- **Impact:** Without this, we can't show you local representatives automatically

#### 2. Voting History (`collectVotingHistory`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We save records of polls you vote on
- **When OFF:** Your votes are counted but not linked to your history
- **Impact:** You won't see your voting history or participation stats

#### 3. Interest Tracking (`trackInterests`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We track hashtags and topics you engage with
- **When OFF:** No personalization based on your interests
- **Impact:** Content recommendations will be generic

#### 4. Feed Activity Tracking (`trackFeedActivity`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We track what you read, like, share, and bookmark
- **When OFF:** Feed interactions aren't saved
- **Impact:** No personalized feed; no read history

#### 5. Analytics Collection (`collectAnalytics`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We collect usage data (clicks, time spent, features used)
- **When OFF:** Only critical error tracking (helps fix bugs)
- **Impact:** We won't know how you use the platform (may affect future features)

#### 6. Representative Interactions (`trackRepresentativeInteractions`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** We track which representatives you follow or contact
- **When OFF:** Interactions aren't saved to your profile
- **Impact:** You won't see your connection history

### Visibility Controls

#### 7. Show Read History (`showReadHistory`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Other users can see what you've read
- **When OFF:** Your reading history is private
- **Impact:** Less public engagement visibility

#### 8. Show Bookmarks (`showBookmarks`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Other users can see your bookmarked content
- **When OFF:** Your bookmarks are private
- **Impact:** Others can't see what you're saving

#### 9. Show Likes (`showLikes`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Other users can see what you've liked
- **When OFF:** Your likes are private
- **Impact:** Your preferences aren't publicly visible

#### 10. Share Activity (`shareActivity`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your civic activity appears in public feeds
- **When OFF:** Your activity is private
- **Impact:** Lower public profile

### Personalization Controls

#### 11. Personalize Feeds (`personalizeFeeds`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your feed is customized based on your interests
- **When OFF:** Generic feed based on trending/recent content
- **Impact:** Less relevant content in your feed

#### 12. Personalize Recommendations (`personalizeRecommendations`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Recommendations based on your activity
- **When OFF:** Generic recommendations
- **Impact:** Suggestions may be less relevant

### Trust & Verification Controls

#### 13. Participate in Trust Tier (`participateInTrustTier`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your trust tier (T0-T3) is visible and affects analytics
- **When OFF:** Your verification level is private
- **Impact:** Can't demonstrate your verification level publicly

### Data Retention Controls

#### 14. Retain Voting History (`retainVotingHistory`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your voting history is kept indefinitely
- **When OFF:** Voting history is periodically deleted
- **Impact:** Lose access to historical voting records

#### 15. Retain Search History (`retainSearchHistory`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your search queries are saved
- **When OFF:** Search history is cleared periodically
- **Impact:** Can't see past searches

#### 16. Retain Location History (`retainLocationHistory`)
- **Default:** OFF
- **When ON:** Your location lookups are saved
- **When OFF:** Location data is cleared periodically
- **Impact:** Need to re-enter location more often

## Data Sharing

### We Never Sell Your Data

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Period.**

### When We Share Data

We only share your data in these limited circumstances:

**1. With Your Explicit Consent**
- When you choose to make information public (e.g., enable "Share Activity")
- When you explicitly share content with others

**2. Service Providers (Required for Operations)**
- **Database Hosting:** Supabase (for data storage)
- **Authentication:** Supabase Auth (for secure logins)
- **Email Delivery:** (If you've opted in to email notifications)

All service providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.

**3. Legal Requirements**
- When required by law or valid legal process
- To protect our rights, safety, or property
- To prevent fraud or security threats

**We will notify you of legal requests unless prohibited by law.**

### We Never Share For Marketing

We do not share your data with:
- Advertisers
- Data brokers
- Marketing companies
- Social media platforms (except when you explicitly share)

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data

**Account Data:**
- Kept as long as your account is active
- Deleted 30 days after account deletion (grace period for recovery)

**Optional Data (Based on Your Settings):**

If retention is **enabled** for a data type:
- **Voting History:** Indefinite (or until you delete it)
- **Search History:** Indefinite (or until you delete it)
- **Location History:** Indefinite (or until you delete it)

If retention is **disabled** for a data type:
- **Voting History:** 90 days
- **Search History:** 30 days
- **Location History:** 7 days

**Analytics Data:**
- Aggregated/anonymized data: Kept indefinitely for platform improvement
- Individual analytics: 1 year (if you've opted in)

### Early Deletion

You can delete any data category or your entire account at any time:
- **Specific Data:** My Data Dashboard → Delete Category
- **All Data:** Account Settings → Delete Account

## Your Rights

### Right to Access
**What:** View all data we have about you  
**How:** My Data Dashboard → View All Data  
**Time:** Instant

### Right to Export (Data Portability)
**What:** Download all your data in JSON format  
**How:** My Data Dashboard → Export All Data  
**Time:** Instant  
**Format:** GDPR/CCPA compliant JSON

### Right to Delete
**What:** Delete specific data categories or everything  
**How:**
- Specific: My Data Dashboard → Delete [Category]
- All: Account Settings → Delete My Account  
**Time:** Immediate (permanent after 30-day grace period)

### Right to Rectify
**What:** Correct inaccurate information  
**How:** Profile Settings → Edit Information  
**Time:** Instant

### Right to Restrict Processing
**What:** Disable specific data collection  
**How:** Privacy Settings → Disable specific controls  
**Time:** Instant

### Right to Object
**What:** Object to data processing  
**How:** Privacy Settings → Opt out of data collection  
**Time:** Instant

### Right to Withdraw Consent
**What:** Revoke previously granted permissions  
**How:** Privacy Settings → Turn off any control  
**Time:** Instant (future collection stops immediately)

### Right to Lodge a Complaint
**What:** File a complaint with a supervisory authority  
**How:** Contact your local data protection authority  
**Also:** Contact us at privacy@choices.app

## Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**Encryption:**
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3+)
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords hashed with industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt)

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication available
- Regular security audits

**Infrastructure:**
- SOC 2 compliant hosting (Supabase)
- Regular backups (encrypted)
- DDoS protection
- Regular security updates

**Development Practices:**
- Open source (community auditable)
- Security code reviews
- Penetration testing
- Responsible disclosure policy

### What We DON'T Do
- Store passwords in plain text
- Use outdated encryption
- Share admin access unnecessarily
- Ignore security vulnerabilities

### If There's a Breach

In the event of a data breach, we will:
1. Investigate immediately
2. Notify affected users within 72 hours
3. Notify relevant authorities
4. Provide clear information about what happened
5. Take steps to prevent future incidents
