# Privacy Policy - Ranked Choice Democracy Platform

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

## Your Privacy Matters

We believe in transparent, user-controlled data practices. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

## What Data We Collect

### Essential Data (Required for Platform Function)
- **Account Information:** Email, username, password (encrypted)
- **Voting Records:** Your poll votes and preferences (anonymous)
- **Representative Data:** Your elected officials and districts

### Analytics Data (Optional - You Control This)
- **Usage Patterns:** Which pages you visit and how you navigate
- **Performance Data:** How fast pages load and any errors
- **Device Information:** Browser type and device for accessibility
- **Geographic Location:** Your state/region for representative accuracy

### Privacy Data (For Your Rights)
- **Consent Records:** What you've agreed to share
- **Data Requests:** When you ask for your data or deletion
- **Audit Logs:** Security and access records

## Why We Collect This Data

### For Democracy & Election Integrity
- **Prevent Bot Manipulation:** Stop automated attacks on polls
- **Ensure Fair Representation:** Verify geographic distribution
- **Maintain Trust:** Protect the integrity of democratic processes

### For Better User Experience
- **Improve Navigation:** Make the platform easier to use
- **Fix Performance Issues:** Speed up slow pages
- **Enhance Accessibility:** Support users with different needs

### For Legal Compliance
- **Meet Regulations:** Comply with privacy laws
- **Protect Your Rights:** Enable data access and deletion
- **Maintain Security:** Monitor for threats and attacks

## Data Protection Measures

### Security
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls:** Strict limits on who can see your data
- **Regular Audits:** Security reviews and updates
- **Incident Response:** Rapid response to any security issues

### Data Minimization
- **Only Necessary:** We collect only what we need
- **Purpose Limitation:** Data used only for stated purposes
- **Retention Limits:** Automatic deletion after specified periods
- **No Cross-Site Tracking:** We don't track you across other websites

### Your Privacy Controls
- **Opt-Out Anytime:** Withdraw consent at any time
- **Data Export:** Download your data in standard formats
- **Account Deletion:** Complete account and data removal
- **Anonymous Usage:** Use the platform without personal data

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Data
- **Analytics Data:** 30 days, then automatically deleted
- **User Profiles:** 1 year, then anonymized
- **Audit Logs:** 90 days, then deleted
- **Consent Records:** 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Voting Records:** Anonymized immediately, no personal connection

### Automatic Deletion
- **Scheduled Cleanup:** Regular automatic data purging
- **Retention Policies:** Clear rules for each data type
- **User Requests:** Immediate deletion upon request
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data retained for compliance

## Third-Party Sharing

### We Don't Sell Your Data
- **No Data Sales:** We never sell personal information
- **No Advertising:** We don't use data for advertising
- **No Cross-Site Tracking:** We don't track you elsewhere
- **Limited Sharing:** Only with your explicit consent

### Service Providers
- **Hosting:** Secure cloud infrastructure
- **Analytics:** Privacy-focused analytics tools
- **Security:** Security monitoring services
- **Legal Compliance:** Data protection consultants

## International Users

### Data Transfers
- **Adequate Protection:** Data transferred only to countries with adequate protection
- **Safeguards:** Technical and contractual protections
- **Your Rights:** Same rights regardless of location
- **Local Laws:** Compliance with your local privacy laws

### Geographic Restrictions
- **US Users:** CCPA compliance
- **EU Users:** GDPR compliance
- **Other Regions:** Local privacy law compliance
- **Universal Rights:** Core privacy rights for all users

## Children's Privacy

- **Minimum Age:** 13 years old to use the platform

- **Immediate Deletion:** Child data deleted upon discovery

### Protection Measures

- **Parental Controls:** Parental oversight options
- **Educational Content:** Age-appropriate civic education
- **Safe Environment:** Child-safe platform features
