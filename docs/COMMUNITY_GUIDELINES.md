# Community guidelines

_Last updated: April 4, 2026_

Choices is a **civic** project: disagreement is expected. These rules keep collaboration and on-platform discussion grounded in **evidence**, **respect**, and **safety**.

## Sourcing

- **Prefer primary sources** — Government filings, official records, and direct links beat screenshots and unsourced claims.
- **Label uncertainty** — If something is alleged or disputed, say so; do not state it as settled fact.
- **No coordinated harassment** — Critique ideas and documented actions; do not organize pile-ons or doxxing.

## Misinformation

- **Correct good-faith errors** — Point to a source or filing; assume people can update their view when shown data.
- **Bad-faith repetition** — Moderators may limit accounts that spam debunked claims after clear correction.
- **Election and health** — Extra care: avoid amplifying uncorroborated fraud or medical claims; defer to official results and qualified sources.

## Moderation stance

- **Procedural neutrality** — We moderate on **behavior and sourcing**, not on which party or ideology someone holds.
- **Escalation** — Repeated violations → warning → temporary restriction → removal, proportional to harm and intent.

## Contributing (code & docs)

- Follow [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md) and [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
- Security issues are **not** handled in public issues; use [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md).

## Contact

Use project issue trackers for code. For sensitive moderation or safety reports, follow channels maintainers publish on the official site or repository.
