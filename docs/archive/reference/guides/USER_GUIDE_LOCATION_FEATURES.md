## üìç Finding Your District

### During Onboarding

1. **Navigate to Demographics Step**
   - Complete the basic information
   - Look for "Find Your District" section

2. **Enter Your Address** (One-Time Use)
   - Type your home address
   - Example: "123 Main St, San Francisco, CA 94105"
   - Click "Look Up District"

3. **Review Your District**
   - We'll show your congressional district (e.g., "CA-12")
   - Your full address is **immediately discarded**
   - Only the district number is saved

4. **Save (Optional)**
   - Click "Save" to remember your district
   - Skip if you prefer maximum privacy
   - You can always add it later!

### From Your Profile

1. **Navigate to Profile Page**
   - Click your profile icon
   - Scroll to "Your District" section

2. **Update Anytime**
   - Enter address ‚Üí Get district ‚Üí Save
   - Or delete your district info entirely
   - You're in complete control!

## üîí Privacy Guarantees

### What We Store
- ‚úÖ Your district only (e.g., "CA-12")
- ‚ùå NEVER your full address
- ‚ùå NEVER your street name
- ‚ùå NEVER your city (only district)

### How It Works
1. You enter address in your browser
2. We send it to Google (one time, over HTTPS)
3. Google returns your district
4. We save the district number
5. **Your address is cleared from memory**
6. Nobody can ever see your address

### Your Rights
- **Optional**: You can skip this entirely
- **Deletable**: Remove your district anytime
- **Exportable**: Included in data export
- **Transparent**: We tell you exactly what we save

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I have to provide my address?
**A:** No! Location features are completely optional. You can use Choices Platform without ever providing an address.

### Q: What if I move?
**A:** Just update your district from your profile page. It takes 10 seconds!

### Q: Can other users see my address?
**A:** **No!** We don't store your address. Only you know your address.

### Q: Can admins see my address?
**A:** **No!** We never save it, so nobody can see it. Not even admins.

### Q: What if I want maximum privacy?
**A:** Don't set a district! You'll see all platform-wide content anyway.

### Q: How accurate is the district lookup?
**A:** Very accurate! We use Google Civic API, which is official government data.

### Q: What about apartments or complex addresses?
**A:** Google Civic API handles all US addresses, including apartments, PO boxes, etc.

### Q: Can I see my representative information?
**A:** Coming soon! Once you set your district, we'll show your representatives.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Address Not Found"
**Solution**: 
- Check spelling
- Include city and state
- Use format: "Street, City, State ZIP"
- Example: "123 Main St, Boston, MA 02101"

### Problem: "Wrong District Returned"
**Solution**:
- Try adding ZIP code
- Be more specific with street name
- Contact support if issue persists

### Problem: "Filter Not Working"
**Solution**:
- Refresh the page
- Disable and re-enable filter
- Check that you've saved your district

### Problem: "No District-Specific Content"
**Solution**:
- That's normal! Most content is platform-wide
- Enable filter to see when district content appears
- Create district-specific polls yourself!
