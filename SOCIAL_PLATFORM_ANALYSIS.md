## Realistic Implementation Strategy

### Phase 1: High-Impact Platforms (Twitter + Facebook)
1. **Twitter**: Full integration with native poll sharing
2. **Facebook**: Rich link previews with Open Graph tags
3. **Focus**: These platforms will drive 80% of your social traffic

### Phase 2: Visual Platforms (Instagram + TikTok)
1. **Instagram**: Generate visual poll cards and representative cards
2. **TikTok**: Create video templates for viral polls
3. **Focus**: Brand awareness and younger demographics
