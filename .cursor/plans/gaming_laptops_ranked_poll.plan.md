Online-only prices still apply regionally (same tax/shipping), but **“incredible” local deals** usually mean **open-box**, **clearance endcaps**, **Costco bundles**, or **price-match** at a superstore—not a separate secret Seattle price list.

### Tax and economics (Seattle purchase)

- **Washington sales tax** applies to most B&M and shipped-to-Seattle purchases (combined state/local often **~10.25%+** in Seattle—**verify current rate** with [WA DOR](https://dor.wa.gov/)). “Incredible deal” comparisons should be **post-tax** when comparing to **OR** border runs (not legal/tax advice).

### How this ties to the poll’s **top-level choices**

- **MSI Vector / HP OMEN / Lenovo Legion Pro / Alienware / RTX 5090 Legion**: Usually **ordered online** (Newegg, HP, Dell, Lenovo) or **Best Buy national SKU**. **Northgate Best Buy** is the best local place to **touch open-box** equivalents of **Best Buy–carried** models (e.g. **HP OMEN MAX** SKU); **MSI Vector** may be **online-only** or limited store stock—**check inventory** on the store page above.
- **“Incredible” hyper-local** wins: **Best Buy open-box** at **330 NE Northgate Way**, **Costco Shoreline** bundle pricing on **Legion** when rotated in, and **price-match** requests (Best Buy policy—read current terms on site).
